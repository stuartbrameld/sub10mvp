Frequently Asked Questions

  • Tell me more about the team

    With some of the brightest individuals spanning London's TechCity and Silicon Valley, the sub10 team comprises individuals across a variety of sectors and industries. We love technology, in particular the thousands of business to business (B2B) startups solving niche business problems in innovative ways at extremely low cost. Above all, we are passionate entrepreneurs. We want to enable our customers to do things differently, in ways they never realised were possible.

  • What if things don't work out?

    All our products and services are software subscriptions. As is typical with cloud and online services, all have free trials and are simple and easy to install. None of our products or services have minimum contracts or require any upfront investment. So, if something doesn't work out, you can simply stop using it. This is as risk free as it gets.

    In terms of sub10 services, our monthly fees spread our costs out over a year yet we have no minimum contracts.   • Can you help with software development?

    Absolutely, we work with a range of well-proven partners to provide competitively priced services to our clients. In the UK we partner with First Option Software, a talented and friendly team based just outside of Winchester. Where clients request offshore development we work with Influxive, whom we have an excellent working relationship with. In order to provide competitive costs to our customers, we do not work with central London design or development agencies.

  • Can we meet some of your partners?

    Absolutely. We'll happily host you at one of the many London events taking place on a weekly basis, including the now infamous Silicon Drinkabout where you can get a taste of what startup life is like. If you'd prefer something a little more personal, we're happy to arrange that as well.

  • A note from us on meetings

    We're a small team, we love business, technology, and spend most of our waking hours discovering, testing and introducing technology to our customers. We don't love meetings. We're happy to have phone calls and video calls, but feel our business is low risk enough for customers to just give us a try, we're sure you'll be pleased you did.
